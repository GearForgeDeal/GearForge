Optional Upgrades (If You Have Extra Budget)

If you can stretch the budget slightly, these add-ons make a big difference:

  • Gaming mouse pad (~€8–€15): A large desk pad ties the setup together and gives the mouse consistent tracking. The Redragon Flick XL is a great pick.
  • Cable management clips (~€6): Nothing ruins an RGB aesthetic like cable chaos. Cheap clips from Amazon fix this instantly.
  • Monitor bias lighting (~€15): Stick an LED strip behind your monitor. Reduces eye strain and looks incredible on stream.
