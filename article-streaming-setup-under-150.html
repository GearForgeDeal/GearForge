Priority Order: What to Buy First

If you're on a tighter budget, buy in this order:

  • 1st: Microphone — bad audio kills streams faster than bad video.
  • 2nd: Headset — so you can hear game audio while streaming.
  • 3rd: Lighting — dramatically improves webcam quality.
  • 4th: Webcam — only after the above are sorted.
