What to Watch Out For at This Price

  • Avoid "7.1 surround sound" claims — virtually no budget headset can do real hardware surround. It's usually just software post-processing.
  • Check the driver size — 40mm is fine for casual use; 50mm or larger improves soundstage for competitive gaming.
  • Mic quality matters more than most people think — if you're playing with friends or streaming, a bad mic makes you sound like you're in a cave.
  • Wired is better under €50 — wireless budget headsets often sacrifice audio quality to hit the price point.
